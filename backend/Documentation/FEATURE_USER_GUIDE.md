## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General
**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter email, and follow OTP verification.

**Q: Can I change my role after registration?**
A: No, user roles are fixed. Contact admin for role-related issues.

### For Students
**Q: Can I register for multiple events?**
A: Yes, you can register for unlimited events.

**Q: How do I unregister from an event?**
A: Go to "My Events", select event, and click "Unregister" (before event starts).

**Q: Where can I download my certificates?**
A: Go to "Certificates" section in your dashboard.

### For Coaches
**Q: Why isn't my event visible to students?**
A: Events require admin approval before becoming visible.

**Q: How long does event approval take?**
A: Usually 24-48 hours. Check notifications for updates.

**Q: What payment methods are supported?**
A: Razorpay supports cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets.

### For Admins
**Q: Can I edit events after approval?**
A: No, only coaches can edit. You can suspend/restart events.

**Q: How do I verify certificate authenticity?**
A: Use "Verify Certificate" feature with unique certificate ID.
